My Favourite Cricketers

Sachin Tendulkar

Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar is an Indian former international cricketer and a former captain of the Indian national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket. He is the highest run scorer of all time in International cricket. He is known as GOD in some places.He scored 100 international centuries.He is one of the cricketing legend and my most favourite cricketer.

David Warner

David Andrew Warner is an Australian international cricketer and a former captain of the Australian national team. A left-handed opening batsman, Warner is the first Australian cricketer in 132 years to be selected for a national team in any format without experience in first-class cricket. He is a hard hitting batsman.He can hit long sixes.He is a treat to eyes.When he is in form,there is no one who can stop him.

Shakib Al Hasan

Shakib Al Hasan is a Bangladeshi international cricketer who has played all three formats of the game. Internationally, Shakib has been recognised as one of the greatest all-rounders of all time. He was ranked as one of the world's most famous athletes by ESPN WORLD FAME 100 in 2019. He is the no. 1 all rounder.He is best in all formats.He is a gem for bangladesh cricket.We all love him a lot.Hope to see him soon on the play ground.
